About Steever Construction

Growing up with and watching a handy, do-it-yourself father is where Zach learned a lot of his skills and aptitude towards home improvement. He also learned quite a bit rehabbing their first home in Cincinnati, OH. Zach further broadened his roofing and carpentry skills working at a Cincinnati based roofing company running the residential side of the business from 2011-2013. Zach’s passion is for fine woodworking but he enjoys all aspects of home improvement.
